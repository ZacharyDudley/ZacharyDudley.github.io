Chrome Extension Manager

One thing I do not like about Chrome: it's not easy to enable/disable extensions. You have to go to the three-dot menu, then to other tools, then extensions, and then find the extension and turn it off.

This extension allows you to see all installed extensions and turn them on or off without having to traverse a single menu. There's also a 'turn all off/on' switch, as well as the ability to lock extensions on or off.
